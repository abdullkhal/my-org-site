About Us

Who we are

The Property Integrity Institute – Kenya (PIIK) is an independent, membership-based professional body established to promote ethical conduct, professionalism, and accountability in real estate agency and property marketing.

Why we exist

Kenya’s real estate sector has grown rapidly, but the absence of consistent professional standards has exposed the public to fraud, misinformation, and unethical practices. PIIK exists to bridge this gap through voluntary certification and public verification.

What we are not

PIIK does not license agents, regulate the industry, or exercise statutory authority. Membership and certification are voluntary and based on adherence to our professional standards.

What We Do

Voluntary Certification

Support ethical practitioners through voluntary certification aligned with professional integrity and transparency.

Public Verification

Enable the public to verify participating practitioners and make informed property decisions.

Standards & Ethics

Promote ethical conduct, accountability, and best practices across real estate agency and property marketing.
